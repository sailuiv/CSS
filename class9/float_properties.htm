World Environment Day

World Environment Day is celebrated on 5th of June every year, and is the united Nations's principle vehicle encouraging awareness and action for the protection of our environment. First held in 1974, it has been a flagship campaign for raising awareness and global warming, to sustainable issues to marine pollution,human overpopulation,Environment Day has grown to become a global platform for public outreach,with participation from over 143 countries anually. Each year, WED has new theme that major corporations,NGOs, commuties, govenments and all celebritites worldwide adopt to advocate environmental causes.
